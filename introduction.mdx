## What is QRcodeer?

QRcodeer is a free, web-based QR code generator that makes creating custom QR codes simple and fast. Whether you need to encode a URL, text, or any other content, QRcodeer provides an intuitive interface with powerful customization options—all without requiring an account or signup.

Built with modern web technologies (React, TypeScript, and Vite), QRcodeer delivers a smooth, responsive experience with real-time preview and instant downloads.

**Simple and fast** - No complicated setup or configuration. Enter your content, customize, and download.

**Privacy-focused** - All QR code generation happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

**Modern and responsive** - Built with the latest web technologies for a smooth experience on any device.

**Free forever** - No hidden costs, premium tiers, or feature limitations. Everything is free to use.
